Articles about PETSc Design¶

Many articles have been written about the design of PETSc. We mention an incomplete selection which may be of interest to developers.

You may also be interested in publications using PETSc.

Higher-level views of PETSc can be found in [ref-Smi11], [ref-BGMS02], [ref-GS94], [ref-BGMS98], and [ref-BGMS97].
